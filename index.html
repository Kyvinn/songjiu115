Get to Know Bali

Travel FAQ

Many travelers need a visa to enter Indonesia. Some nationalities can apply for a Visa on Arrival, while others may need to apply before arrival.

The dry season is usually the most popular time. May, June, and September are often considered good months because the weather is pleasant and crowds are smaller.

Canggu is popular for surfing and cafes, Ubud for culture and nature, Seminyak for restaurants and nightlife, and Sanur for a calmer family-friendly stay.

Scooters are common for short distances, while private drivers are better for longer day trips. Ride-hailing apps and taxis are also widely used.
